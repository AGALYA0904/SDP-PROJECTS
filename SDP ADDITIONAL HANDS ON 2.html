Intresting Facts about Rabbits

Rabbits, also known as bunnies or bunny rabbits, are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha. Oryctolagus cuniculus includes the European rabbit species and its descendants, the world's 305 breeds of domestic rabbit.They can live up to 12 years. They are masters of hearing. They have almost 360 degeree vision. They communicate using secret code. The baby rabbits are called as 'Kittens'. They need lots of space to stay healthy. They are very clean. They are very social.
